Family Finance Tracker - Overview

A Family Finance Tracker helps families manage income, expenses, budgets, and savings efficiently. It provides a shared platform for multiple family members.

How It Works

  • User Roles & Accounts: Family Head oversees finances; members track their expenses.
  • Income & Expense Tracking: Log income sources and categorize expenses.
  • Budgeting & Goals: Set monthly budgets and define savings goals.
  • Reports & Insights: View graphs and alerts for overspending.
  • Multi-User Collaboration: Family members contribute to shared expenses and savings.

Benefits

  • Transparency: All members can see and plan expenses.
  • Better Budgeting: Prevents overspending.
  • Goal-Oriented Savings: Helps achieve financial milestones.
